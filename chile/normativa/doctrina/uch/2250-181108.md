# El Partido Comunista de Chile y los pueblos indígenas entre 1912 y 1973: continuidades y cambios en el discurso

**Autor(es):** Urzúa, José Miguel

**Año:** 2021  **Tipo:** Artículo de revista

## Resumen

This paper presents the results and conclusions of the study of the relationship between the ideas that were elaborated from PC of Chile with respect to the indigenous peoples, especially Mapuche. Through a hemographic review, it identifies and analyzes the discourses established by the PC; with the purpose of verifying the hypothesis regarding the elaboration of different inclusive speeches to understand the existence and political participation of the different indigenous peoples, from 1912 to 1973.
